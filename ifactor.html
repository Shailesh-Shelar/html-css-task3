Mobiles and tablets have evolved the way we engage in websites.It's now less about clicking and and more about scrolling, swiping and tapping,Long-Scrolling websites takes away the barrier of complex navigation,providing a responsive user friendly experince for mobile users.

EASIER FOR USERS TO REALISE THEIR GOAL


These day scrolling is so simple that it's become an instinctive action,whereas clicking through to another page requires thought and motivation. A single page website allows user to identify their purpose on the website quicker,than needing to slift through multiple pages.

LONG SCROLL: THE NOT SO GOOD STUFF

YOU NEED TO BE SEARCH ENGINE SHAVY

One of the biggest problem that parallex websites face is that they are not SEO friendly.WHY? When you limit the number of pages on your websites. Google has fewer pages to index and negatively impacts your standing on the search engine results(SERP).Luckily there are ways around this.

SLOWER LOAD TIMES

50% of website users bounce off the page if it hasn't loaded in 3-5 seconds.So the more high resoluation imagery and animation you include, the longer it takes to load pages When writing this aticle,we tested some popular parallex websites,and found that the ones with complex visuals took up to 30 secounds to load on mobile device, whereas simple and minimalist long scroll websites loaded within few seconds.

AWKWARD NAVIGATION

Insted of hitting the "back" button,users must scroll back up the page which can be frustering for users who want to refer back to certain element. An easy way to around this is to include top navigation and have each element anchored to a category or section.
